Just some ideas to keep you 
comfortable for the whole day.

Attire

For our special day, we invite our guests to join us in celebrating with a dress code that blends elegance and comfort. 

Given the summer heat and our beautiful outdoor venue on a gentle slope, we recommend opting for light, breathable fabrics and comfortable shoes.

Ladies might want to consider flats or sandals instead of heels to ensure ease and enjoyment throughout the day. Gentlemen can stay cool with light dress shirts and trousers.

Colours

Our wedding will be a vibrant celebration, so wear any colour the makes you feel fabulous - even white!

Feel free to add personal touches and express your unique style. We're even running a fun challenge: if anyone manages to outshine the bride, there might be a special prize (hint: it's excommunication!)

So let your fashion sense shine and add to the joy of our special day.

RSVP

Please RSVP separately for each member of your family who will be attending.

Your love and support mean the world to us, and we ask for nothing more than your presence as we begin this new chapter in our lives.
